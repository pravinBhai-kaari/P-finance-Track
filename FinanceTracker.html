📈 Step-Up EMI
Start with a lower EMI and increase it every year as your salary grows. Great for young earners.
Example: 5% step-up means your EMI increases by 5% each year. At ₹50,000 starting EMI → ₹52,500 in year 2 → ₹55,125 in year 3...
💳 Extra EMI / Monthly Prepayment
Pay extra principal every month to reduce tenure and save interest. Even small amounts help.
Example: Paying ₹5,000 extra per month on a 20-year loan can reduce tenure by 3–5 years and save lakhs in interest.

Lump Sum · SIP with Step-Up · SWP Withdrawal Planning

💡 Invest a fixed amount monthly. Step-Up SIP increases your monthly amount every year as income grows.
💡 SWP — withdraw a fixed monthly amount from your corpus while the rest keeps growing. Used for retirement planning.
